About the Association

Mission: Promote faith, love, and social justice by supporting vulnerable communities.

Vision: To be a reference in humanitarian, spiritual, and sustainable development actions.

Values: Faith, solidarity, respect, commitment, and transparency.

History: The Missionary Association was born from a dream of solidarity. Over the years, it has impacted countless lives through education, agriculture, and faith projects.
